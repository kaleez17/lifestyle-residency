About Us

A Sanctuary in the Heart of Chennai

Nestled in the cultural heart of Thiruvanmiyur, Lifestyle Residences offers a rare blend of contemporary comfort and timeless elegance. Our serviced accommodation is designed for the discerning traveller who expects more than just a room.

Steps from the iconic Kalakshetra Foundation and the serene seafront, we are ideally positioned for both leisure and business stays. Each space is thoughtfully curated with premium amenities, ensuring every moment feels like an indulgence.
